Project Overview

This project analyzes employee performance across various departments, job roles, and demographics. It explores the impact of training, experience, and job category on overall ratings. The interactive dashboard offers valuable insights to help management identify performance trends, improve productivity, and design data-driven workforce strategies for long-term success.
